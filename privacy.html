Privacy Policy

WatchWorkout · Last updated: June 15, 2026

The short version: WatchWorkout does not collect, store, or transmit your personal data or health information to any server. Everything stays on your devices, in your Apple Health database.

We have no user accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party tracking.

What WatchWorkout Does

WatchWorkout is an Apple Watch app that lets you pin workout types as tiles for one-tap starts. When you start a workout, the app records it directly to your Apple Health database using HealthKit. That data never touches our servers — we don't have any.

Health Data (HealthKit)

WatchWorkout uses Apple's HealthKit framework to record workout sessions. During a workout, the app reads and writes the following data types to your personal Apple Health database:

  • Workout type (e.g. Running, Strength Training)
  • Workout duration
  • Active energy burned (calories)
  • Heart rate

This data is stored solely in your Apple Health database on your devices and in iCloud, under Apple's own privacy and security controls. WatchWorkout does not transmit, upload, or share your health data with any external server or third party.

We will never use HealthKit data for advertising, marketing, or any purpose other than providing the workout-tracking functionality you see in the app.

Workout Tile Preferences

Your pinned tile configuration (the list of workout types you have selected) is stored locally on your Apple Watch and iPhone using standard device storage. This list is synced between your Watch and iPhone using Apple's WatchConnectivity framework — directly between your own devices, not through any server. Your tile preferences are not accessible to us.

In-App Purchases

WatchWorkout offers a one-time Pro upgrade through Apple's App Store. All payment processing is handled entirely by Apple. WatchWorkout does not receive, store, or have access to your payment information. Purchase status (whether you have unlocked Pro) is verified through Apple's StoreKit framework on your device.

No Data Collection or Analytics

WatchWorkout contains no analytics, crash-reporting, or advertising SDKs. We do not collect usage data, device identifiers, or any other information about how you use the app.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with anyone. There is no data to share — nothing leaves your devices.

Children's Privacy

WatchWorkout does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app does not collect personal information from anyone.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the date at the top of this page. Significant changes will be noted in the app's release notes.
